Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2025  ·  Effective immediately
01
Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate game servers and improve the Zanshin experience. This includes:

  • Basic gameplay telemetry (match results, mode played, session duration)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data to identify engine bugs
  • Account identifiers provided by Epic Games Store or Itch.io platforms
  • IP address for matchmaking region detection (not stored long-term)
We do not collect: real names, payment details, physical addresses, or any biometric data. Platform purchases are handled entirely by Epic Games or Itch.io under their own privacy frameworks.
02
How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Server matchmaking and region balancing
  • Leaderboard calculation and ranking
  • Identifying and resolving bugs in the combat engine
  • Aggregate analytics to improve game balance

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers under any circumstances.

03
Data Retention

Gameplay telemetry is retained for a maximum of 12 months, after which it is anonymised or deleted. If you close your account, all personally identifiable data is removed within 30 days. Crash reports are retained for 90 days for debugging purposes.

04
Your Rights

Depending on your region you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Submit requests to the contact address below. We aim to respond within 30 days.

To exercise your data rights, contact us via the Zanshin Discord server (#support channel) or through your platform's reporting system.
05
Cookies & Website Tracking

Our website uses basic analytics cookies to understand site traffic. No cross-site tracking or advertising cookies are used. See our Cookie Policy for full details. You may opt out at any time through the banner provided on first visit.

Community Rules
Last updated: April 2025  ·  Applies to Discord, in-game chat, and all official Zanshin spaces
01
The Code of the Blade

Zanshin is built on the values of precision, respect, and discipline — both in combat and in community. Every member of this clan is expected to uphold these principles.

Treat every opponent as a worthy adversary. The greatest dishonour is not losing a duel — it is behaving without dignity.
02
Prohibited Conduct
  • Harassment, personal attacks, or sustained targeted behaviour toward any player
  • Hate speech based on race, gender, religion, nationality, sexual orientation, or disability
  • Sharing personal information of other players without consent (doxxing)
  • Intentional game-ruining: AFK, throwing, or deliberate sabotage of teammates
  • Exploiting game bugs without reporting them to the dev team
  • Impersonating developers, admins, or other community members
  • Spam, excessive self-promotion, or unsolicited advertising
03
Enforcement

Violations are handled by the moderation team with the following framework:

  • Minor breach — Verbal warning in channel
  • Repeated minor / moderate breach — Temporary mute or 7-day suspension
  • Severe breach — Permanent ban from Discord and game servers

All ban decisions are final unless appealed within 14 days through the #appeals channel in Discord.

04
Reporting

To report a violation, use the in-game report system or post in the #reports channel on Discord with evidence. Reports are reviewed within 48 hours. False reports submitted in bad faith may result in action against the reporting party.
